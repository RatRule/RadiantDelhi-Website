ABOUT US



WHO ARE WE AND WHAT WE DO?

Established in the year 2002,in New Delhi (India), we are engaged in distributing a comprehensive assortment of Industrial Adhesives & Sealants, Surface Protective Coatings, Metal Filled Epoxies, Anti-Seize Compounds, Biodegradable Cleaners, etc. of Loctite® make and Specialty Lubricants of Molygraph® make.

These products are formulated by our principals under the guidance of their skilled professionals by making use of best grade chemicals and latest processing techniques.
The offered products are highly acclaimed for their properties like purity, high effectiveness, longer shelf life, precise pH value and accurate composition.

OUR VALUES

Radiant is dedicated to providing the best Quality, Value and Service to meet our customers’ needs. At Radiant, our business principles are rooted in our personal faith and beliefs.Solving problems instead of selling products has been our business moto since the year of establishment.

OUR MISSION

We aim at achieving higher customer satisfaction by giving utmost care in consistency of quality of our delivered products.Our long standing relationship with our customers & principals have helped us in achieving what we have today. Maintaining these good relations as well as establishing new ones is the primary goal of our organisation.
